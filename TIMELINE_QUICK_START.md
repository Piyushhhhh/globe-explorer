# ⚡ Temperature Timeline - Quick Start

## 🚀 In 30 Seconds

1. **Open browser**: http://localhost:8000/globes/temperature/
2. **Wait 2 seconds** - Timeline appears at bottom
3. **Click "▶ Play"** - Watch Earth warm up!

That's it! 🌍🔥

---

## 🎯 5-Minute Demo

### Perfect First Experience

**Step 1:** Click **"📜 1880"** button
- Jumps to Industrial Revolution
- Globe mostly blue/green (cool temperatures)
- Temperature: -0.16°C

**Step 2:** Set speed to **"Slow"**
- Bottom left of timeline
- 200ms per year = detailed viewing

**Step 3:** Click **"▶ Play"**
- Animation starts automatically
- Watch years advance
- See colors change in real-time

**Step 4:** Watch the transformation
- **1880s-1950s**: Blue/green (cool)
- **1960s-1980s**: Yellow appears (warming starts)
- **1990s-2000s**: Orange spreads (clear warming)
- **2010s-2023**: Red intensifies (crisis!)

**Step 5:** See dramatic changes
- **Russia/Canada** turn red first (Arctic amplification)
- **Europe** shifts from green to orange
- **USA** gradually warms yellow → orange
- **Global** color shift very visible

Total time: ~5 minutes (worth every second!)

## 🌡️ What to Watch For

### 1. Arctic Turns Red First
- **Russia** - Watch turn orange → red
- **Canada** - Same rapid warming
- **Greenland** - Extreme change
- Shows "polar amplification"

### 2. Europe Warms Steadily
- **1880s**: Cool greens
- **1950s**: Starting to yellow
- **1990s**: Mostly orange
- **2020s**: Red patches appearing

### 3. Acceleration After 1980
- **Before 1980**: Slow color changes
- **After 1980**: Rapid transformation
- **After 2000**: Very fast warming
- **2010-2023**: Crisis visible

### 4. Global Pattern
- **Arctic**: Fastest (red)
- **Mid-latitudes**: Fast (orange)
- **Tropics**: Slower (yellow/orange)
- **Southern Ocean**: Slowest (blue/green)

## ❓ Troubleshooting

**Timeline doesn't appear?**
- Wait 2 seconds after page loads
- Refresh page (Cmd+R or Ctrl+R)
- Check server is running on port 8000

**Animation choppy?**
- Use slower speed
- Close other tabs
- Try different browser

**Can't click year?**
- Drag slider instead
- Use keyboard arrows
- Click preset buttons

## 📊 What The Numbers Mean

### Temperature Anomaly
- **Negative (blue)**: Cooler than baseline
- **0°C**: Baseline (1951-1980 average)
- **Positive (yellow/orange/red)**: Warmer than baseline
- **+1.35°C (2023)**: Crisis level

### Why 1.35°C Matters
- **1.5°C** - Paris Agreement safe limit
- **2.0°C** - Dangerous warming threshold
- **We're at 1.35°C** - Very close to 1.5°C!
- **Time is running out** - Urgent action needed
