## 📱 Responsive Design

### Breakpoint Strategy

| Device | Width | Layout | Cards | Typography |
|--------|-------|--------|-------|------------|
| **Mobile** | <768px | Single column | 1/row | 36px title |
| **Tablet** | 769-1024px | 2 columns | 2/row | 48px title |
| **Desktop** | >1025px | 3 columns | 3/row | 60px title |

### Fluid Typography
Uses `clamp()` for smooth scaling:
- Title: 36px → 60px (based on viewport)
- Descriptions: Proportional scaling
- Maintains readability at all sizes
